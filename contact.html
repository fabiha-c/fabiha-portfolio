About Me

I'm a senior at NYU Tandon School of Engineering pursuing a B.S. in Biomolecular Science with a minor in Public Policy & Management. As a GLASS (Global Leaders and Scholars in STEM) Scholar, I've dedicated my undergraduate journey to exploring oral health inequity as a global challenge.

My experiences span clinical work as a dental assistant in underserved NYC communities, research at NYU Abu Dhabi and NYU Dentistry, and global clinical exposure in Sri Lanka and Kenya. I'm passionate about culturally competent, disability-inclusive dental care and the intersection of global health policy and clinical practice.

I'm currently preparing to apply to dental school with the goal of becoming a clinician who addresses oral health disparities through patient-centered, equitable care.
